Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about admissions, programs, and campus life

Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks (40% for reserved categories). They must also qualify NEET-UG with the required cutoff score. Age should be between 17-25 years as of admission date.

The annual tuition fee for MBBS is 15,00,000. This includes tuition, library, laboratory, and examination fees. Hostel accommodation is available at an additional cost of 1,50,000 per year. Scholarships and financial aid are available for meritorious and economically disadvantaged students.

Yes, we have separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with capacity for 3000+ students. Rooms are available in single, double, and triple-sharing options. All hostels are air-conditioned and equipped with high-speed WiFi, 24/7 security, mess facility, gymnasium, and recreational areas.

Students receive extensive clinical training at our 1200-bed teaching hospital starting from the first year. They rotate through 20+ departments including OPD, IPD, Emergency, OT, and ICU. Annual patient footfall of 500,000+ ensures diverse case exposure. Additionally, community medicine postings are conducted at rural health centers.

Yes, we offer various scholarships including Merit Scholarships (up to 100% tuition waiver), Need-based Financial Aid, Sports Scholarships, and Research Fellowships. Government scholarships and bank education loans can also be availed. Approximately 25% of our students receive some form of financial assistance.

Our graduates have excellent career prospects. 70% pursue higher studies (MD/MS/PG Diploma), 20% join government hospitals, and 10% work in private healthcare or start their practice. Our alumni work at prestigious institutions like AIIMS, PGI, Johns Hopkins, Mayo Clinic, and leading corporate hospitals across India.
